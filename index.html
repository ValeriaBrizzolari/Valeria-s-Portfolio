About me


I'm Valeria, a self-taught aspiring Learning Designer with a strong interest in creating accessible and engaging online learning experiences. I recently started exploring digital tools like H5P, Vevox, and Canva to build interactive content, and I'm teaching myself the basics of platforms like Moodle and Adobe Creative Suite. My background includes hands-on experience running my own business, managing logistics and customer service, and more recently, learning web development through a coding course. Now, I'm excited to apply my tech skills and creativity to the world of digital learning.
