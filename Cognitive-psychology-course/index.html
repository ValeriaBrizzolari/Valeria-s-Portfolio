Welcome to Cognitive Psychology 101

Discover how the human mind processes information, makes decisions, and stores memories. This course introduces you to the fascinating world of cognitive psychology—exploring topics like attention, perception, memory, and problem-solving. Whether you're new to psychology or looking to deepen your understanding, you're in the right place.

Modules

Attention and Perception

Explore how we focus our attention on specific stimuli and how our brain interprets sensory information. Understand the processes that help us perceive the world around us and the factors that influence our attention.

Find out more

Memory and Learning

Dive into the science of memory and the different types of memory systems. Learn how we acquire, store, and retrieve information, and explore key theories behind learning and memory retention.

Find out more

Problem-Solving and Decision Making

Discover how we solve problems and make decisions in our daily lives. Understand cognitive biases, decision-making strategies, and the role of critical thinking in overcoming challenges.
